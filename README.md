# Skinwalker
Something that not only looks like you but also copies and does your exact movements. Touching it will result in instant death.
## Multiplayer
In Multiplayer. Each player has their own Skinwalker. Contact with other player's Skinwalkers won't kill unless it's with your own Skinwalker.
## Difficulty
### Normal
Skinwalker deals 10 damage upon contact. (5 Hearts)
### Hard
Skinwalker deals 15 damage upon contact. (7.5 Hearts)
### Nullscaper
Skinwalkers have halved spawn time. Two additional skinwalkers spawn. Skinwalkers always kill regardless of health upon contact.
## Settings
**#Value skinwalker.delay** | _100_ | Ticks till spawn after movement.\
**#SpawnValue skinwalker.delay** | _20_ | Ticks till an additional Skinwalker.\
**#Value skinwalker.amount** | _1_ | Skinwalkers to spawn when initializing.
### Requirements
Minecraft 1.21.9+
